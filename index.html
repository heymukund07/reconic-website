About us

Reconic Esports is a mobile esports organization based in North America founded in 2022. We strive to connect players from all around the world by creating opportunities for casual and competitive players. Our priority on community is a foundational value that has persisted since Royal Gaming, the organization that started it all. We look to maintain this with the rebrand to Reconic Esports by keeping the same goals and values. The name also stands for it with a combination of Royal Esports (Re) and Iconic. We always aim to compete and succeed in the industry. Our competitive programs improve and bring out the best in our players. We support and provide opportunities for our community with consistent giveaways and tournaments catering to all types of people. And, we create a platform for players from all over the world to connect and grow together. Welcome to Reconic Esports!
